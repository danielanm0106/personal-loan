What is an online personal loan?

An online personal loan is a financial product that you can apply for and complete entirely through mobile devices such as phones, tablets, or computers. The whole process usually takes just a few minutes, and upon approval, the funds are often disbursed instantly into your account. You can use the money for various purposes according to your needs.

What is this loan for?

You have the flexibility to choose the purpose of the loan. Whether it's for home renovations, vacations, covering medical expenses, consolidating debts, or any other personal goal. The loan amount ranges from $3,000 to $20,000, and you can choose a repayment term of up to 36 months with a fixed interest rate.

What is the interest rate for this loan?

The interest rate for this personal loan ranges from 9% to 18%, depending on the loan amount and amortization period. It's important to note that with a fixed interest rate, your payment remains consistent throughout the loan. If you'd like to estimate the rate, you can click here to access the loan calculator.

Do I need insurance for an online personal loan?

Yes, insurance is required for a personal loan. Life insurance is mandatory as it covers the outstanding balance of your loan in the event of unexpected circumstances such as death, disability, or critical illness. You can select the insurance option that best suits your needs from your preferred provider, or you can add it directly to your online loan application.
